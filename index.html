Unravel the new dimensions

Startup Mahostav is a global scaled hackathon encompassed to bring in an Indo-Germanic reciprocation. The event is a two-day virtual hackathon rooted with intention and determination to solve the real-world problems/challenges within the time frame. The expected participants of the event are proficient computer programmers, software developers, graphic designers, domain experts, tech and startup enthusiasts.

Themes

Agritech

Designing device/software with an objective to enhance the life of agrarians and improve the quality of life.
Medicaltech

Designing device/software to aid in managing the healthcare sector in efficient ways.
Food technology

Creating futuristic and unconventional solutions to solve the challenges faced in application of food science.

About

Startup Mahostav is an international Indo-Germanic hackathon initiated by two esteemed institutions namely, SRH University Heidelberg and Amal Jyothi College of Engineering. The 48-hours long live event will be conducted in three gripping phases. Participants are admitted as a team, each consisting of four members.
