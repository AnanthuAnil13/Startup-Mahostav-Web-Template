About

Startup Mahostav is an international Indo-German hackathon initiated by two esteemed institutions namely, SRH University Heidelberg and Amal Jyothi College of Engineering. The expected participants of the event are proficient software developers, tech and startup enthusiasts, etc.

International Level

The participants will be from mainly 2 nations, India and Germany.

Clash of Teams

Participants are admitted as a team, each consisting of four members. 2 from India and 2 from Germany.

Participate Virtually

The event is a two-day virtual hackathon rooted with intention and determination to solve the real-world problems/challenges within the time frame.
